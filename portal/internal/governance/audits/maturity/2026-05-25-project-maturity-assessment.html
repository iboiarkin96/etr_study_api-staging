Overview

Assessment type: project (engineering programme) maturity. Formation date: 2026-05-25.
Compare the engineering programme to good practice (Table 1), score current state (Table 2), and track prioritized gaps (§ Scoring summary).

Scope and methodology

Scope

In scope: architectural governance (ADRs, RFCs), delivery (PR review, CI), reliability (SLOs, runbooks, on-call), security (auth, secrets, supply chain), observability (logs, metrics, traces), and people (onboarding, role progression). Out of scope: product strategy, individual SLA reviews, and dimensions already covered by API / DX / UI-UX audits.

Methodology

Scores assigned by reading the ADR/RFC corpus, sampling 10 recent PRs, inspecting CI configs (.github/workflows/), the SLO dashboard, the on-call rota, secret-scanning policy, and the role-progression matrix in the team handbook. Each Table 2 row carries a justification. Final score is a weighted average across six axes.

Scoring summary

Narrative overall

Overall: about 6.0 / 10.
To move toward top decile: formalise error budgets, run quarterly DR drills, ship an SBOM + signed artifacts, and bake threat modelling into the RFC template.
