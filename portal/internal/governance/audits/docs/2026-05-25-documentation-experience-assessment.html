Overview

Assessment type: DX (documentation experience). Formation date: 2026-05-25.
Compare the portal to top-decile docs programs (Table 1), score current state (Table 2), and track prioritized gaps (§ Scoring summary).

Scope and methodology

Scope

In scope: the internal Diátaxis quadrants (Tutorials, How-to, Reference, Explanation), navigation tree, search, design tokens / UI Kit v3, authoring templates, CI gates (make verify, docs-spec-check), and the feedback widget. Out of scope: the public marketing site and product UI, which sit under separate audits.

Methodology

Scores assigned by walking the four Diátaxis quadrants, sampling 5 pages per quadrant, inspecting scripts/validate_docs_* outputs, the nav-tree-internal.json mock, and the most recent feedback batch. Each Table 2 row carries a justification. Final score is a weighted average across six axes.
