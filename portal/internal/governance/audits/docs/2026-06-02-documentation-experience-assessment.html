Overview

Assessment type: DX (documentation experience), v2 «Architect's Bar» format. Formation date: 2026-06-02. Snapshot taken against main at commit 562756b; previous baseline 2026-05-25.
Headline: high discipline (A−) paired with newly-substantive content (B). The Vol. 2 push (BL-052…063) closed twelve substance gaps; the remaining cap on the score is operational liveness — closing the feedback loop, growing the postmortem corpus from real incidents, and measuring time-to-first-PR.

Scope and methodology

Scope

In scope: the internal Diátaxis quadrants (Tutorials, How-to, Reference, Explanation), the new onboarding/ hub (cold-start paths), navigation tree, search, design tokens / UI Kit v2 (assets_v2/), authoring templates (handbook/sa/templates/), CI gates (make ci, make docs-check, scripts/validate_docs_*, scripts/spec_consistency.py, .github/workflows/a11y-formal-checks.yml), the auto-mounted feedback & edit-on-github widgets, governance backlog, and the new substance corpus (architecture narrative, three postmortems, threat model, capacity snapshot, data invariants, 30 ADRs, 4 RFCs). Out of scope: public marketing site and product UI.

Methodology

Three tracks, cross-linked inside one HTML file: (A) the same 25-row rubric as 2026-05-25 with a Δ column added; (B) ten architect-bar axes graded A+ … F with a verdict paragraph, three buckets (A-level / B-level / C–D-level), severity-coded gaps, and exemplars; (C) three phases tied to gap severity + a backlog cross-analysis (take / defer / add) + a concrete 4-week plan. Every score names a file, ADR, RFC, script or workflow; where the claim rests only on visual inspection it is marked not-confirmed.

Summary verdict

~B+ on the architect's scale (improved from ~B− in the May cycle). High discipline (Diátaxis, ADR quality, tooling, UI Kit v2, contract gating). Substance pulled up from C+ to B/B+ in one cycle thanks to the Vol. 2 push — postmortem corpus, STRIDE threat model, capacity numbers, architectural narrative, 30/30 ADR alternatives audit, per-persona cold-start. The remaining gap to A− is not more content — it is operational liveness: a feedback loop that closes end-to-end, an SLO burn-history that grows weekly, a time-to-first-PR number that exists, and a search ranker that prefers titles.
