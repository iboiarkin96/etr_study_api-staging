The five governance trees

  1. 01 · What & why · 31

    Architecture Decision Records

    One short, numbered file per architectural decision. Captures context, the choice, and consequences — so the team does not re-litigate the same questions.

    Open→
  2. 02 · How & details · 5

    Requests for Comments

    Implementation specifications and design rationale that an ADR intentionally omits. Where the how lives once the what is decided.

    Open→
  3. 03 · What was reviewed · 11

    Audit reports

    Dated architecture, quality, documentation, UI/UX, and maturity assessments per ADR 0024 — architecture and quality assessment documents.

    Open→
  4. 04 · What's queued · single source

    Backlog

    One list for everything planned, in flight, blocked, or shipped — across product, documentation, infra, and quality.

    Open→
  5. 05 · What is proven · 13 · 5 sev-1

    Traceability matrix

    One row per commitment, traced left-to-right: owner · ADR · API operation · test · runbook. Blank cells are work items, not styling gaps. Methodology lives in the SA reference handbook.
