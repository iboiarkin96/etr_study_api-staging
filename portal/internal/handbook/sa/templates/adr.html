Alternatives considered MANDATORY · MIN 2

Substance gate. An ADR without at least two named rejected alternatives is not auditable — six months later nobody remembers the constraint set, so the decision cannot be revisited without re-running the whole analysis. Each rejected alternative carries a one-line «rejected because…». See Tradeoff disclosure for the canonical shape; see ADR 0008 (Make taxonomy), ADR 0020 (PlantUML), and ADR 0029 (DB docs) as exemplars.

If memory is genuinely lost on a historical ADR — record «historical: alternatives not captured at decision time» and flag for the next semiannual ADR audit pass; do not fabricate rejected options.
