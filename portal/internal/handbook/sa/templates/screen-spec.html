Mobile acceptance checklist

Run on iPhone 17 Pro Max (≈440 CSS px) and iPhone SE (375 CSS px). The full normative list lives at UI motion & adaptivity § Mobile contract; this section captures the screen-specific items.

  • No body-level horizontal scroll on the captured screen states.
  • Body prose computed font-size ≥ 15 px on mobile.
  • Each table either fits the column or scrolls horizontally inside its .docs-table-scroll wrapper.
  • Sticky page-title row does not occlude the first paragraph after the H1.
  • Mobile screenshot captured at 390×844 (or device-frame equivalent) and stored next to the spec.
