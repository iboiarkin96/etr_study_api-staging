Lens chip

Diátaxis lens identifier for handbook pages. Five lenses (stance · rules · rationale · apply · sources) compose a Foundations page; each section heading carries one chip so readers see which lens to apply. Three lenses intentionally reuse the colour of their Diátaxis quadrant elsewhere on the page: rules → warn, rationale → info, apply → success. Stance is solid-filled (highest emphasis). Sources is muted (provenance footer).
