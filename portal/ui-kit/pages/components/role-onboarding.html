Role onboarding

Page-template component. Two coupled patterns that ship together: (a) a role-hub intro with two symmetrical callouts (.role-callout--radar + .role-callout--onboarding) pointing to the radar and the onboarding journey, and (b) a 60-day onboarding journey timeline (.role-onboarding) on the dedicated onboarding page. Used by all six SDLC role portals (SA · Architect · Dev · Manager · QA · SRE).

Anatomy

Hub intro

  • .role-hub-intro — plain typographic title sheet with a subtle left-edge rule. Deliberately NOT a card — the two callouts below are the cards, the intro is the section heading + lede.
  • .role-callout--radar — large clickable callout linking to the role's radar page. Has a stat row (5 axes / 25 practices) and a mini-radar SVG visualisation in the corner.
  • .role-callout--onboarding — sibling callout linking to the role's onboarding journey. Same shape, accent tone, mini-timeline visualisation in the corner.

Onboarding journey

  • .role-onboarding — outer wrapper for the dedicated onboarding page.
  • .role-onboarding__milestone — one step in the journey (typically 6 milestones: Week 1, Month 1, etc.).
  • .role-onboarding__when — mono time-horizon label («Day 1», «Month 1», …).
  • .role-onboarding__deliverable — what the engineer can do after this milestone — the bar to meet.
  • .role-onboarding__reads — bullet list of canonical pages to read for this milestone.

CSS namespace

All selectors live in services/frontend/portal/assets_v2/ui-kit/components/role-onboarding.css (724 lines, 19 selectors). The file ships in entry.css. Classes are unprefixed (.role-hub-intro, .role-callout, .role-onboarding) — no .role-onboarding-page body class needed; the selectors are specific enough that they don't collide with other role-* utilities.

Adding a new role

  1. Copy internal/team/roles/<existing-role>/index.html to internal/team/roles/<new-role>/index.html — that's the hub with the two callouts.
  2. Copy internal/team/roles/<existing-role>/onboarding.html alongside.
  3. Edit role name, lede, and the six milestone blocks (when / deliverable / reads).
  4. Update the mini-radar / mini-timeline SVGs inside the callouts so they hint at the right shape.
  5. Register both pages in nav-tree-internal.json under team/roles/<role>/.

No CSS edits — the template inherits everything from role-onboarding.css.
