Tone-rail variants

Use .docs-card--rail with a data-tone attribute to encode the card's category at a glance — Diátaxis quadrants, lifecycle stages, per-kind blocks, tradeoff wins / costs. Tones available: accent · info · warn · success · teal · rose · muted. Add .docs-card--rail-tinted for a soft tone-wash background.

data-tone="accent"

Accent

Default brand colour. Pair with primary actions, the "what to do next" card.

data-tone="info"

Info

Cool / informational. Used for descriptive panels and system kinds.

data-tone="success"

Success

Positive outcome, tradeoff "wins", green-trunk gates.

data-tone="warn"

Warn

Cost, caveat, deprecation stage, "look up" quadrant.

data-tone="teal"

Teal

Library kind, secondary surface. Distinct from info.

data-tone="rose"

Rose

Anti-patterns, group kind, attention without alarm.

States

  • Default — --shadow-xs, 1 px --line border.
  • Hover (link card) — --shadow-md, translateY(-2px), border switches to --accent.
  • Focus — accent outline 2 px, offset 2 px.
  • Disabled — opacity 0.5, pointer-events none.

A11y

  • A link card must be a single <a> — do not wrap the whole card in another link or button.
  • Title is an <h3> by default; pick the right level for your page outline.
  • Decorative arrows in footer must be inside aria-hidden spans or be CSS pseudo-elements.
  • Ensure pills in head don't truncate before the title text.

Tokens used

  • --card, --line, --shadow-xs, --shadow-md
  • --accent (hover border)
  • --radius-md, --radius-lg
  • --space-4 (padding)
  • --ease-out (lift transition)
