States

A11y

  • Always set type="button" when not inside a form; otherwise it implicitly submits.
  • Icon-only buttons must have aria-label.
  • Visible focus ring is required — never remove without a replacement.
  • Disabled buttons use the disabled attribute, not pointer-events: none; otherwise focus order breaks.
  • Tap target ≥ 40 × 40 px on mobile.

Tokens used

  • --accent, --grad-accent (primary)
  • --bg-elev, --line (secondary)
  • --text, --muted (ghost)
  • --radius-md, --radius-full (icon)
  • --space-2, --space-3, --space-4
  • --shadow-xs, --ease-out
