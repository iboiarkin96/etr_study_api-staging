SA glossary banner

Split CTA banner with a soft accent→info gradient — prose blurb on the left (h3 + p), CTA button on the right. Used to point a reader from a deep-dive page to an adjacent reference (glossary, related ADR, sister handbook page). Collapses to a single column on mobile.

Example

Need a term defined?

The portal glossary covers every domain noun used across the SA reference family — system, component, library, API, resource, plus the lifecycle and freshness states.

Open glossary

Anatomy

  • .cta-banner — 2-column grid (prose 1fr + CTA auto), large padding, soft linear-gradient(135deg, accent 5%, info 3%) over --bg-elev.
  • h3 inside — small bottom margin so the paragraph sits right under it.
  • p inside — 60ch max-width, --muted.
  • CTA — any .docs-btn variant; aligned to the row's vertical center via align-items: center on the grid.

Markup

<aside class="cta-banner" role="complementary">
  <div>
    <h3>Need a term defined?</h3>
    <p>The portal glossary covers every domain noun …</p>
  </div>
  <a class="docs-btn docs-btn--primary" href="…">Open glossary</a>
      </aside>

Responsive

  • ≥761 px — two columns, CTA right-aligned, vertically centered.
  • ≤760 px — single column; CTA stacks below the prose, full-width (inherits .docs-btn's mobile rule).

Tokens used

  • Layout: --space-1 / -5, --radius-lg.
  • Surface: --bg-elev, --line.
  • Tone: --accent, --info (both mixed at 3–5% over --bg-elev for the gradient).
  • Typography: --muted.
