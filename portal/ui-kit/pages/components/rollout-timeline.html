Rollout timeline

Two-column phased timeline: a mono «WEEK 1»-style rail on the left, step title + one-line hint on the right. Use for ordered time-boxed steps — rollout plans, sprint walkthroughs, migration phases.

Example

Week 1

Wire the pipeline

CI job runs the migration on a copy of staging; no traffic shift yet.

Week 2

Backfill in batches

Idempotent batches of 10k rows, daily; monitor lag + error rate.

Week 3

Cut writes over

Feature flag flips at 5% → 25% → 100% over 48 hours with SLO watch.

Week 4

Drop the old code path

Delete the legacy reader; archive the migration script; close the ADR.

Anatomy

  • .pa-timeline — bordered wrapper, 2-column grid (90px · 1fr).
  • .pa-timeline__week — left rail label; mono uppercase, tone-tinted.
  • .pa-timeline__step — right column step; small accent left-border so the steps read as a track.
  • .pa-timeline__title — semibold step title.
  • .pa-timeline__hint — muted one-liner.

Markup

<div class="pa-timeline">
  <span class="pa-timeline__week">Week 1</span>
  <div class="pa-timeline__step">
    <p class="pa-timeline__title">…</p>
    <p class="pa-timeline__hint">…</p>
  </div>
  <!-- repeat for each step -->
      </div>

Naming note
