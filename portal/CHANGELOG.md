### Fixed

- **Diagram lightbox — global listeners no longer leak across page lifetime (F-01 from 2026-05-09 bug audit):** [`services/frontend/portal/assets/docs-diagram-lightbox.js`](assets/docs-diagram-lightbox.js) — the lightbox previously created its full UI tree at module load and registered `window.mousemove`, `window.mouseup`, and `document.keydown` once for the entire page lifetime, even on internal pages without any `img.diagram--uml`. No behavior change when the lightbox is open — the visible UX (pan, zoom, keyboard shortcuts, focus restoration) is preserved.
